## 3. Headline conclusion

**Constants** (CV < 5%): `z1`, `z2`, `x_in`, `rms1_out`, `x_mid`, `rms2_out`, `q`, `gate`, `up`, `softmax_safety_scale`, `pre_finsmx_mean`

**Stable-but-varying** (5% ≤ CV < 15%): `scores`, `h`, `o`, `mlp_out`

**Genuinely varying** (CV ≥ 15%): `o_mean`, `mlp_out_mean`

**Verdict — idx=0 safety**: idx=0 falls **outside** ±50% of the population median for: `o_mean`, `mlp_out`, `mlp_out_mean`. These are where the 1.5× margin may be insufficient for some layers.
